Classic Caesar Salad

⏰ 20 Minutes 🍳 Serves 2 Easy Veg
← Back to Recipes

The Caesar Salad is a timeless classic that has earned its place on tables around the world. Crisp, cold romaine lettuce dressed in a bold, creamy Caesar dressing loaded with garlic and lemon, topped with golden homemade croutons and generous shavings of Parmesan, creates a perfectly balanced dish. It works equally well as a starter, side dish, or a light main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • 80 g Parmesan cheese, shaved
  • 2 thick slices sourdough bread
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, halved
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced (dressing)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prepare the croutons first. Tear or cut the sourdough bread into rough 2 cm chunks. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Rub the pan with the halved garlic clove, then add the bread pieces. Toast for 5 to 7 minutes, turning frequently, until deep golden and crunchy on all sides. Season with a pinch of salt and set aside to cool.
2
Make the Caesar dressing by whisking together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and minced garlic in a small bowl until smooth and well combined. Add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and whisk again. Season generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Taste and adjust the lemon or seasoning as needed.
3
Wash and thoroughly dry the romaine lettuce leaves. Tear or chop them into large, bite-sized pieces and place in a large mixing bowl. Cold, dry lettuce is essential for a great Caesar, so chill the torn leaves in the refrigerator for 10 minutes if time allows.
4
Drizzle the dressing generously over the lettuce leaves and toss well until every leaf is evenly coated. Transfer to serving plates or a large bowl. Scatter the cooled croutons over the top, then add generous shavings of Parmesan. Finish with a few extra grinds of black pepper and serve immediately.
